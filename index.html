Tourny Maker

Tourny maker is an application where i kept in mind people that like to create amateur soccer tournaments. Creating a tournament and registering teams is very simple. It doesn't only crete torunaments but manages them as well, keeping track of points, creates a calendar where every team will play each other. In case of a mistake the tournament creator can go back to the match and update the result without altering total points or total goals scored.
